# Inclusion Log

Records all curation decisions: documents added, documents considered and
rejected, and the rationale for each decision.

---

## How to use this log

For each decision, add an entry with:
- Date
- Document (title, authors, year)
- Decision: INCLUDED or EXCLUDED
- Rationale
- Curator
